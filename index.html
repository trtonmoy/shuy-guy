How to Buy Pokemon

You can buy $SHY on any DEX which supports Solana liquidity pools. We recommend using the Jupiter , or Raydium.


  • 1

    Create Wallet

    Install Phantom and create a Wallet on your desktop or mobile device. Your wallet will let you buy, sell, transfer, or receive $SHY.

  • 2

    Send SOL to Your Wallet

    Buy SOL on any cryptocurrency exchange of your liking and transfer your SOL to your wallet.

  • 3

    Connect Wallet to the Solana

    If $SHY isn't already listed, click on “select token”, paste the $SHY contract address, and select Pokemon INU.

  • 4

    Swap SOL for $SHY

    Now go ahead and proceed with the swap. Your $SHY tokens will be added to your wallet. (If you can’t yet see your new tokens on Metamask, click on “import tokens” -> “custom token” and paste the $SHY contract address)
